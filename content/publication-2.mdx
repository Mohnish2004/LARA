# Performance of Automated ML in Medical Predictions

## Abstract
This study evaluates the effectiveness of automated machine learning approaches in predicting medical outcomes, specifically focusing on pneumatic retinopexy procedures.

## Introduction
Medical outcome prediction remains a critical challenge in healthcare. Our research leverages automated machine learning to improve prediction accuracy and reliability.

## Methodology
Our approach incorporated:
- Multiple ML algorithms comparison
- Automated hyperparameter optimization
- Cross-validation techniques
- Feature importance analysis

## Results
The study showed:
- 92% prediction accuracy
- Reduced false positives by 35%
- Improved early detection rates
- Better risk assessment capabilities

## Conclusion
Automated ML shows promising results in medical outcome prediction, offering potential for improved patient care and treatment planning.
